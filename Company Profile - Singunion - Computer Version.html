Singunion was founded by Thomas Yang and Benny Hu in 1996. Based in Singapore, we have been doing shipping business globally — establishing ourselves as a professional, efficient and accountable company in the shipping community. 公司由 Thomas Yang（杨）与 Benny Hu（胡）于1996年共同创立，立足新加坡，业务遍及全球。二十余年来，在航运界建立了专业、高效、值得信赖的口碑。

Equipped with ship-owning and ship management experience, we understand our clients' needs and expectations more thoroughly, providing Ship Agency and Ship Chartering services across the globe. 凭借丰富的船东及船舶管理经验，我们深刻理解客户的需求与期望，立足新加坡，放眼全球，为各地客户提供优质的船务代理及船舶租赁服务。

Our Services 服务范围

01
⚓

Ship Chartering船舶租赁

Freight & shipping services for commodity traders — strong ties with shipowners, operators and international traders worldwide.为大宗商品贸易商提供租船及货运服务，与全球众多船东、运营商及国际贸易商建立了深厚合作关系。

02
🚢

Ship Agency船务代理

Comprehensive port agency for all vessel types — cargo ops, bunkering, repairs, SNP delivery & all port activities at Singapore.为各类船舶提供全面的港口代理服务，包括货物操作、加油、维修、船舶买卖交接及所有港口相关事宜。

03

General & Bulk Cargoes Operation普货或散货操作

Full operational support for general and bulk cargo vessels — coordination, documentation, scheduling and field supervision.为件杂货、钢材、设备、散杂货船舶提供完整的操作支持，涵盖协调、单证处理、排期及现场监督。

04
👥

Crew Management船员管理

Sign-on/off, crew changes, ICA immigration processing, visa documentation and crew welfare services.提供船员上下船、换班、移民局手续、签证文件及船员福利等全方位管理服务。

05
🛡️

Husbandry / Owner's Protective Agent船东保护代理

Acting as Owner's Protective Agent — safeguarding vessel interests, coordinating supplies, spares, repairs and P&I liaison.担任船东保护代理，维护船舶利益，协调物料供应、备件、维修及与P&I保险机构的沟通。

06
📋

Documentation & In/Outward Clearance单证处理及进出口清关

B/L and cargo documentation handling, MPA submissions, dangerous goods declarations and full in/outward port clearance.处理提单及货物文件，办理MPA申报、危险品申报及完整的进出口通关手续。
